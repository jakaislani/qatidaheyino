Pat Corrales cause of death: What happened to him?

The baseball community is mourning the loss of a true icon, Pat Corrales, who passed away at the age of 82 on August 27, 2023. Corrales, a beloved figure known for his multi-faceted contributions to the sport, left this world peacefully at his residence in Big Canoe, Georgia, succumbing to natural causes.

Throughout his remarkable career, Corrales wore many hats – from player to manager to coach – amassing an impressive legacy that spanned over six decades. His influence and passion for the game were felt deeply by players, fans, and colleagues alike. As the news of his passing ripples through the baseball world, tributes pour in to honor a life that has forever left its mark on the sport.
