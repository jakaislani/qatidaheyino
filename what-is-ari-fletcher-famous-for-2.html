What is Ari Fletcher famous for?

Ariana Fletcher was born on July 12, 1995, in Chicago, Illinois. Her ethnicity is African-American, and her zodiac sign is Cancer.

Her older brother, Kyle “K.J.” Jamison, passed away in 2013. Her brother, Fletcher, was quite close to her. As an homage, she renamed all of her social media accounts ‘Kyle’ after his passing. She shared a video on Instagram on the eighth anniversary of her brother’s death, August 30, 2021.

What is Ari Fletcher famous for?

Ariana Fletcher is a Chicago, Illinois-based Instagram model, social media influencer, and businesswoman.

Fletcher rose to prominence as a result of her connection with rapper G Herbo. Her attractive appearance, cheerful demeanor, and a carefree attitude have garnered her both lovers and opponents. Despite the fact that she is no longer in a relationship with G Herbo, her new relationship with rapper Moneybagg Yo has garnered a lot of attention.

In an interview, Fletcher explained that her entrepreneurial instincts came from her childhood. When Fletcher was younger, she had the opportunity to be among people with great business abilities.

This exposure benefited her in starting her own business. Fletcher first used Instagram in 2014. “Of course, my first image was of my angel and me,” she wrote her first post, a snapshot of her with her brother. She’s since gone viral on Instagram for her amazing modeling images.

Fletcher is currently one of the top Instagram influencers, and she has worked with a number of large businesses. Her hair brand, “Kyche Extensions,” was created in 2016.
Fletcher established her YouTube channel in November 2016, where she generally posts vlogs, usually in the form of episodes.
