Alexander Draper Wolff, popularly known as Alex Wolff. He was born on 1st November 1997, hails from America. He is not just an actor, yet an adorable singer and musician as well. He earned recognition for appearing in the Nickelodeon musical comedy series titled ‘The Naked Brothers Band’  started in 2007 and ended in 2009. In the series, he worked alongside his older brother, Nat Wolff. The series was directed by their mother named Polly Draper. He along with his brother Nat dropped two music albums for the series ‘The Naked Brothers Band’. The duo formed a band named ‘Nat & Alex Wolff’ and released two music albums titled ‘Black Sheep’ in 2011, and Public Places’ in 2016. The boys were also featured in their mother’s directorial comedy-drama film ‘Stella’s Last Weekend’which was released in the year 2018.

Wolff focused on his career life and started making many appearances in films, such as; ‘Dzhokhar Tsarnaev in Patriots Day (2016) and John ‘Derf’ Backderf in ‘My Friend Dahmer’ in 2017, and many more that are worth sharing. Also, he tried his luck in the field of direction and made his debut through the drama film named ‘The Cat and The Moon’ in 2019.

Career

The young actor started off his career at the age of 6 in the year 2005. He debuted through a musical comedy-drama film named ‘The Naked Brothers Band’ which was written and directed by his mother. In the film, he acted alongside his older brother Nat. The Nickelodeon series got immense popularity. It was produced by his mother, Dolly Draper. On the other hand, Alex along with his brother Nat released music for both the film and series. His father produced and supervised the music.

Alex Wolff also released two music albums and the song titled ‘Crazy Car’ which secured 83rd position on the Billboard Hot 100 Charts.

Apart from this, he also featured in the ‘Fall Out Boy’ music video ‘The Take Over, The Breaks Over’ Wolff made a cameo performance in the Nickelodeon TV Movie named ‘Mr. Troop Mom, and many others that are worth watching. In ‘Mr. Troop Mom’, Wolff worked alongside George Lopez, Daniela Bobadilla, April Telek
Elizabeth Thai, Jessica McLeod, Pyper de Marsh, Geoff Gustafson, and Jane Lynch.

In addition, he was featured in a horror film named ‘Hereditary’. It seems he is a versatile actor who has the ability to fit himself in each role. However, the young star made his career broad and got huge acclaim.

Achievements

Wolff won the Broadcast Music, Inc, Cable Award in 2007 for the film and series titled ‘The Naked Brothers Band’. Among others, he won the Audience Award for a Family Feature Film at the Hamptons International Film Festival in 2005, and many others are on the list.
