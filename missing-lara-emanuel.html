Missing Lara Emanuel: Know Everything About this Incident

Lara Emanuel, a woman from Morris County, has been reported missing by her family members after not being seen for two days. The police have appealed to the public for assistance in locating her, emphasizing the urgency of the situation.

Missing Lara Emanuel:

Family members and friends are urgently seeking assistance in locating Lara Emanuel, a woman from Morris County who has been missing for two days. The family reported her disappearance, and the police have appealed to the public for help in finding her. Lara Emanuel resides in Roxbury and was last seen on Wednesday, June 7, 2023. Descriptions provided indicate that she is approximately 5 feet 6 inches tall with red hair, and she weighs around 125 pounds. The Morris County Prosecutor’s Office has released a statement stating that Lara Emanuel, 46, has not been seen in Roxbury since Wednesday, and her car was discovered at the Holiday Express in Mount Arlington on Thursday morning. The search for Lara Emanuel continues as concerned individuals join forces to bring her home safely.

Authorities are urging anyone who possesses information regarding the whereabouts of Lara Emanuel or can provide assistance in the ongoing investigation to come forward. Individuals can reach out to the Roxbury Police Department by dialing 973-448-2100 or contact the Missing Persons Unit On-Call Detective at the Morris County Prosecutor’s Office through the Morris County Communications Centre by calling 973-285-2900.
