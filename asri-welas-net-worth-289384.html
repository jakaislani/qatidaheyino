Indonesian television actress, model, presenter, radio announcer and dancer known for her long career and range of roles. She has starred in popular TV shows like Husbands Fear Wives, Forty Days, and 2020's Chef Ball. She also appeared in the feature films Ghost Writer 2, I Know When You Die, and Triangle: The Dark Side. Her asri_welas Instagram account has 650,000 followers, and her Asri_Welas Twitter account has 550,000 followers.

She began dancing at the age of two. She graduated from the Faculty of Social and Political Sciences at the Universitas Indonesia. She majored in Psychology.

She taught dance at her studio called Sanggar Asri Welas.

She married her husband, Galiech Rahardja, in 2007. She has four children named Rajwa, Gilbram, Ridha, and Rahardja. Her older brothers are Indrawan and Puguh Yuniantoko.
