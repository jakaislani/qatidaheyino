The base salary of the Walmart CEO is just one component of their overall compensation package. In addition to a fixed salary, the CEO typically receives performance-based bonuses, stock options, and other incentives that can significantly increase their total earnings and net worth.

Stock options are a common form of compensation for CEOs, providing them with the opportunity to purchase company stock at a predetermined price. Bonuses and Incentives

These bonuses can be tied to various metrics, such as revenue growth, profitability, or stock performance, and can make up a significant portion of the CEO’s total compensation.
