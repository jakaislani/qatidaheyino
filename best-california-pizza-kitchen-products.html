All the California Pizza Kitchen Products, Ranked

The best California Pizza Kitchen products taste like the real thing you’d get at the restaurant. I find that there are a lot of CPK haters out there and I do not understand why. Their crust is great. They make the best restaurant ranch in the business. Their pizzas are cooked in a big oven in front of your eyes! That’s really artisan stuff right there. I love California Pizza Kitchen as a restaurant.

Prior to this taste test, I’d tried one or two of the California Pizza Kitchen products you can buy at the grocery store. I liked them! I did a little research and discovered that they have a ton of pizzas and salad dressings at the grocery store. We tried every single one of the California Pizza Kitchen products we could get our hands on and ranked them from best to worst. See where your favorite CPK product lands!

CPK!

Ranch DressingWalmart

This was the biggest disappointment. If you’re a CPK lover like myself, you know that they have some of the best ranch dressing on planet Earth. Sadly, this tastes absolutely nothing like the truly euphoric ranch you get in the restaurant. This tastes bland and weird and chemical-y. These CPK dressings are a collab with Litehouse salad dressing, which we love! I am so confused by this bad ranch. Do not buy this; buy one of the best ranch dressings instead.

Credit: Merc / Walmart

Rating:

0/10
