Triston McKenzie Debuts On MLB With Cleveland Indians

Triston McKenzie made his professional Major League Baseball (MLB) on Saturday (22 August 2020) with Cleveland Indians, where his performance was of elite level. McKenzie struck out 10 in six high-velocity innings against the Detroit Tigers at Progressive Field to give his team a great victory of 6-1.

The 23-year-professional played his debut match against the Tigers after the Indians promoted him to the active roster. It means that he made his way to the Indians' 40-man roster.
