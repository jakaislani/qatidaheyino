Who is Vera Wang? Age , Net-Worth

THE NAME Vera Wang is known for its style, designs, and celebrity clientele among shoppers and pop culture fans.

Vera Wang, Vogue’s former Senior Fashion Editor, has been the designer behind her famous self-titled clothing line for decades.

Who is Vera Wang?

Vera Wang rose to prominence as the head of her eponymous fashion label, but becoming the head of her clothing brand was not her first career choice.

Vera was a professional ice skater before becoming a celebrity fashion designer.

She competed in the 1968 US Figure Skating Championships and was featured in Sports Illustrated in January of the same year, despite her pro-ice skating ambitions.

She opened her first boutique in New York City in 1990, after working at Vogue and Ralph Lauren.

Vera has created red carpet looks and wedding gowns for many celebrities over the course of her career.

The Kardashians, Ariana Grande, Mariah Carey, Victoria Beckham, Michelle Obama, Michelle Kwan, and many others have been among her previous clients.

How old is Vera Wang?

Vera Wang is 73 years old.
she was born on June 27, 1949 in New York.

To commemorate her 73rd birthday, the figure skater-turned-designer took to social media to share details about her celebrations.

Vera shared several photos on Instagram while wearing a pink gown she designed and a tiara.

She captioned one of her posts while promoting her wine brand, “Celebrating my Bday with CAKES and KARAOKE…… and my new ROSÉ PROSECCO!”

What is Vera’s net worth?

Vera’s net worth is estimated to be $500 million, according to Forbes.

Her fortune stems from her earnings as the sole owner of her clothing line.
