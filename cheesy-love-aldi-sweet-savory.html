WAYS TO SAVE AT ALDI

While Aldi is known for being one of the most affordable major supermarkets, there are ways you can boost your savings while shopping there.

One approach is to download an app like Lozo or Brickseek which locates all the best sales in your area at Aldi and other stores.

Other apps like Ibotta give you cashback rewards for purchasing certain items.

Another tactic is to be on the lookout for Aldi's Finds.

Each week Aldi releases a new batch of heavily discounted items.

These products can range from workout gear to blenders.

And whenever you go shopping, it's usually beneficial to make up a list of essentials you are there to buy.

This helps you steer clear of non-essential impulse buys even Aldi strategically places throughout the store to get you to spend more than you'd like.
