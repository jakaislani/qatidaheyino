Sydney Sweeney Is Out of This World In Moon Boots Shop The Dupes Now

In fashion, what goes around comes back around — and moon boots are making their way back into orbit. Moon boots were at the height of winter fashion in the 1980s, with the original inspiration derived from the 1969 Apollo 11 Moon Landing. Since then, Moon Boot has collaborated with some notable top brands, like Jimmy Choo and Chanel. The chunky shoe is known for its creative patterns and extra padded insulation to mimic that of an astronaut’s gear — and it seems that your favorite celebrities are doing their part to keep the trend alive.

Sydney Sweeney was the latest to be spotted in the brand with her Instagram post dedicated to year-end. The Anyone but You actress sported the $320 Icon Faux Curly Cream Boots on her latest trip to the grocery store, pairing them with an oversized fuzzy jacket in a similar fabric and fitted black leggings. The shoe also comes in black and is advertised to be water-repellent faux sheepskin with a gender-neutral design.

The Euphoria star isn’t the only celeb to hop on the moon boots trend. Rihanna was pictured de-boarding a private jet earlier in the year (typical icon behavior from the singer) in the $470 Icon Low Cream Faux-Fur Beads Boots. The lunar landing-inspired boots have a drawstring at the top and a slip-on design (because, let’s be real, slip-on shoes are superior to laces—we said what we said!).

While most of us don’t have the extra cash to spend on high-end shoes or a private jet — one can dream! — we did some data to find an identical version once again. Steve Madden’s Mav Fashion Boots for women are available on Amazon and sell for $84.95. You can snag them in the color bone, similar to Ri Ri’s, or go bold with a pink or tan multi-color. She styled the boot with a faux fur cheetah print coat, a forest green beanie, and a comfy gray sweatsuit. Like we said, typical icon behavior!
