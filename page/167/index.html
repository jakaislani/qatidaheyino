(1949-1963) Who Was Addie Mae Collins?On September 15, 1963, Collins and three other African-American girls were killed in a terrorist attack perpetrated by members of the Ku Klux Klan. This crime marked a turning point in the Civil Rights Movement. Three men responsible for Collins' murder were brought to justice between 1977 and 2002.
