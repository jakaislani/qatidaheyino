8 Things You Didnt Know About Don Benjamin

➡ Don Benjamin Biography

Don Benjamin is an American television personality and a social media celebrity who became a finalist on season 20 of America’s Next Top Model. Also, he appeared in Ariana Grande’s music video for the song “Into You”. Alongside this, he has worked on television and in the field of music, became widely popular among fans and followers on social media. Well, still, his fans want to know more about him so here we are going to share some unknown things you didn’t know about him.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Don Benjamin

Before fame

Before fame, Don Benjamin was a model and has modeled for various, top class, brands, such as; Guess, Tillys, and Pink Dolphin.

His family background

Don Benjamin was born in Chicago, USA where he raised and grew up, later he moved around a lot growing up, with different cities in Minnesota, Florida, and Mississippi.

Don Benjamin & Liane Valenzuela

Don Benjamin and Liane Valenzuela started dating in 2015 and got engaged in the year 2019, but later, they parted their ways and shared a video named “Opening Up About Our Break Up” in March 2020.

He runs a YouTube channel

Don Benjamin runs collaboratively YouTube channel with his love lady, Liane Valenzuela in 2015. The duo named their YouTube channel ” Forever Us”.

He started his career in music

In 2012, Don started his music career with the song named “Across the Sky” and since then, his music has been featured on “Big Brother”.

He is a great actor as well and made an acting debut

Don’s skills are not confined to just modeling, yet he is also a great actor who made his acting debut with television series named “One Ten”. He appeared in an episode of “One Ten” in the year 2014.

He & social media

Don Benjamin became the most active celebrity on social media and he uses almost all social media accounts including Twitter, Instagram, Facebook, and also runs a YouTube channel as well. He has a huge fan following with over millions of followers and subscribers.

His hobbies

In his off-days, he loves to watch movies, and also work out to keep himself in a shape. Alongside this, being a model, he takes care of his overall diet and nutrition to remain healthy and fit.
